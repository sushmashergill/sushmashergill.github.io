Challenge

Cabinet Warehouse faced a fragmented digital presence for its garage cabinet solutions. The challenge was to design a dynamic platform that showcased customizable storage options while simplifying the user journey from product exploration to inquiry.

Goal

The goal was to develop a responsive website with visually striking product displays, clear CTAs, and an intuitive layout. The design needed to highlight product features while streamlining the browsing and inquiry process.

Result

The new platform transformed user engagement, delivering a smooth, mobile-friendly experience. Customers could easily explore storage solutions, configure products, and request quotes, driving higher conversions and brand credibility.
